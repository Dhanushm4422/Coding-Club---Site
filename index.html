ABOUT US

Everybody should learn to program a computer because it teaches you how to think.

As the Coding Club of a technical institution, we oversee a plethora of coding-related activities including events, workshops, query sessions, and contests, to name a few. We adhere to the belief that "Coding, like poetry, should be short and concise." Spreading knowledge and fostering a competitive coding culture lies at the core of our club's existence. We provide a mutual support platform for students and guide them through their programming journey.

While the club has been functioning relatively well compared to others, there is still room for improvement. The current secretaries have initiated some commendable efforts, such as forming smaller groups focused on specific areas (competitive programming, development, and machine learning) and implementing a mentorship program for freshmen. However, there are still many areas where the club can enhance its operations. We are determined to build upon this momentum and strive for further progress.
